Cucumbers: Quick Pickles

If you have a bunch of cucumbers and want to make sure they don't go bad, an easy thing to do is preserve them by pickling!

This method is done on the stovetop, is stored in the refrigerator, and does not require canning. Quick and easy!

Ingredients:
  • 4 c cucumbers (about 2 regular sized cucumbers)
  • 1 c water
  • 1 c vinegar (I prefer rice wine vinegar)
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 3/4 c sugar
Directions:
  1. Peel and thinly slice cucumbers, discarding the ends.
  2. Loosely pack cucumber slices into a glass jar (an old sauce jar works great for this!).
  3. In a medium sized pot, combine water, vinegar, salt, and sugar. Bring to a simmer.
  4. When simmering, pour pickling liquid into jar over the cucumber slices, making sure to cover all the cucumbers.
  5. Seal the jar, and place in the fridge. Pickles will be ready when the jar is fully cooled.
