Eleanor Castracane, Ph.D.

I am a National Research Council Postdoctoral Associate at NAWCWD China Lake, studying photodegradation kinetics of PFAS using boron nitride nanotubes.

My work and research interests include mass spectrometry, photoelectron and vibrational spectroscopy, and instrument development.

I am also an adjunct professor in the Science Department at Cerro Coso Community College, where I teach general chemistry.
